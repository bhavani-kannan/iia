O2C Transformation: Agentizing Order to Cash

A discrete manufacturing company running SAP across order, inventory and finance modules - with a third-party live delivery tracker and Excel-based regional sales planning - transforms its end-to-end Order to Cash process through the Foundation Accelerator.

Overview

A discrete manufacturer with a mature SAP landscape but fragmented visibility across the O2C chain. Sales targets live in Excel, delivery data sits outside SAP, and exception handling is largely manual across teams.

Sector
Discrete manufacturing enterprise
Multi-region operations with structured order management, fulfilment and finance functions running on SAP
Objective
Agentize end-to-end Order to Cash
From regional sales planning through order fulfilment, delivery, invoicing and cash collection - with intelligent agents at every stage
Complexity
Three disconnected data sources, one process chain
SAP modules do not surface delivery status from the third party tracker. Excel sales plans are manually reconciled against actuals. Exceptions managed across teams without a unified view.
Existing System Landscape
SAP SD SAP MM SAP FI 3rd Party Delivery Tracker Excel Sales Business Plan

Where the Process Breaks Today

Eight friction points across the O2C chain that automation directly addresses. Each represents a stage where manual effort, disconnected systems or reactive behaviour creates cost and risk.

01 Credit Management
Credit decisions are static and manually governed
Credit limits are static with no adjustment for actual payment behaviour. Holds are managed manually with no risk scoring, delaying good orders and missing bad risk.
02 Sales Planning
Regional targets locked in Excel
Sales targets live in Excel and are reconciled against SAP actuals manually at month end. No live variance view or automated alert when a region drifts from plan.
03 Order Management
Order exceptions handled reactively
Stuck orders need manual triage across teams to diagnose and resolve. No prioritisation by revenue at risk or SLA deadline - every exception looks the same.
04 Delivery Tracking
Live delivery data invisible to the business
Live delivery status sits in the third-party tracker with no feed into SAP. Teams react to delays after the fact - never before the customer is impacted.
05 Invoice Quality
Billing errors create rework before cash is even collected
Pricing mismatches and billing errors are not caught before invoice dispatch. At 5-10% error rates on complex orders, each correction extends DSO and feeds the disputes queue.
06 Collections
Collections driven by aging reports, not risk
Collections run from static aging reports with no scoring by default probability. High-risk accounts get the same attention as low-risk ones.
07 Deductions and Disputes
Customer deductions managed on spreadsheets
Customer deductions are tracked manually on spreadsheets with weeks-long resolution cycles. At 2-4% of sales, the revenue leakage is material but largely invisible to the business.
08 Cash Application
Manual payment matching creates close delays
Incoming payments are matched to open invoices manually in SAP FI. Partial payments and reference errors generate exception volumes that delay the monthly close.

Why a full deployment is required here

O2C intelligence spans three disconnected source systems - SAP, a third-party tracker and Excel. Unlike a single-system agent deployment, cross-stage insight (linking a delivery delay to AR risk to collections priority) requires a shared data foundation. The six-layer approach is not overhead here - it is what makes the agents coherent across the process chain.

O2C Process Architecture

Each stage of the O2C process transitions from its current manual or disconnected state to an agent-supported future state. Agents do not replace human decisions - they eliminate the manual effort around them.

Six sequential stages, each with a dedicated intelligence and agent layer. Cross-stage signals - credit risk gating order entry, delivery risk feeding collections priority, deduction patterns feeding dispute resolution - are enabled by the shared data foundation.

O2C Intelligence Pod

Three tiers of intelligence applied across the O2C chain. Predictive models surface risk and variance before they become problems. Prescriptive recommendations tell teams what to do about them. Agentic automation removes the manual effort entirely where human oversight is not required.

PRESCRIPTIVE
AGENTIC
Customer Credit Risk Scoring
Score each customer continuously on payment default probability using AR aging trends, days sales outstanding history and order pipeline exposure - refreshed on every payment event.
Business value: Credit decisions are grounded in live risk signals, not periodic manual reviews. High-risk accounts are identified before orders are placed.
Credit Limit Adjustment Recommendations
When risk score changes materially, recommend the appropriate credit limit adjustment or hold action to the credit controller - with the supporting evidence and expected impact on exposure.
Business value: Controllers act on evidence, not instinct. Limit decisions are faster, consistent and defensible.
Credit Risk Agent
Monitors payment behaviour and AR signals across the customer base. When a threshold is breached, surfaces the credit recommendation to the controller and - upon approval - triggers the limit or hold update in SAP FI automatically.
Business value: Eliminates manual credit review cycles. Every at-risk account is assessed in near real time with a full audit trail of every decision.
Order Fulfilment Risk Scoring
Score each open order on delivery risk using current inventory levels, production schedule status and carrier performance data from the third-party tracker.
Business value: Prioritise at-risk orders before they breach customer SLAs and escalate to disputes.
Order Exception Prioritisation
Rank all stuck orders in SAP SD by revenue at risk, customer SLA proximity and days blocked - with a recommended resolution action for each.
Business value: Teams work the right exceptions first. No high-value order slips through while low-value ones are resolved.
Order Exception Agent
Monitors SAP SD continuously for stuck orders. Diagnoses root cause - credit hold, inventory, pricing - drafts resolution steps and routes to the responsible owner for approval before any action is taken.
Business value: Eliminates manual exception triage. Every stuck order is seen, diagnosed and actioned - with a full audit trail.
Delivery Delay Prediction
Model live tracking signals, carrier history and route data from the third-party system to predict which in-transit shipments are likely to arrive late.
Business value: Enable proactive customer communication and reduce claims and penalties from late deliveries.
Delivery Re-routing Guidance
When a shipment is flagged at risk, surface the best alternative carrier and routing options ranked by cost, speed and availability for dispatcher decision.
Business value: Logistics teams act on options immediately rather than spending time identifying them manually.
Delivery Risk Agent
Monitors live third-party tracking data continuously. On delay detection, flags the shipment, surfaces rerouting options and notifies the logistics coordinator for a decision - before the customer is impacted.
Business value: Closes the visibility gap between the third-party tracker and the business. At-risk deliveries are caught and escalated in minutes, not days.
AR Default Risk Scoring
Score all open invoices daily by late payment and default probability using customer payment history, invoice age, dispute patterns and macro signals.
Business value: Collections effort concentrates on the accounts that matter most, not the ones at the top of an aging report.
Regional Plan vs Actuals Variance Alert
Compare live SAP sales actuals against ingested Excel plan targets by region. Flag deviations above threshold and recommend reforecast or reallocation actions to sales leadership.
Business value: Sales leaders see regional performance against plan in real time - not at month-end when it is too late to act.
Sales Plan Ingestion Agent
Reads uploaded Excel Sales Business Plan files, validates structure and regional target data, normalises formats and loads confirmed targets into the platform for live comparison against SAP actuals.
Business value: Eliminates the manual effort of reconciling Excel plans against SAP. Regional targets are live in the platform within minutes of upload.
Deduction Validity Prediction
Score each short payment on the probability that the deduction is valid versus disputable - using claim type, shipment records, invoice history and prior resolution patterns for that customer.
Business value: Teams focus dispute effort on the deductions most likely to recover cash rather than processing the full queue uniformly.
Dispute Routing and Resolution Recommendations
For each deduction, recommend the optimal resolution path - credit memo, short shipment adjustment, pricing correction or escalation - with the relevant supporting documents retrieved and attached automatically.
Business value: Deduction handlers receive a pre-built case file rather than spending hours gathering evidence. Resolution cycle time cut from weeks to days.
Dispute Resolution Agent
Detects deduction patterns in incoming payments, classifies claim type, retrieves supporting documentation and routes each case to the responsible team with full context and a recommended action. Tracks resolution status and escalates stalled cases.
Business value: Eliminates the spreadsheet-driven deduction queue. At 2-4% of sales, systematic resolution of unwarranted deductions delivers direct revenue recovery.
Cash Application Match Rate Prediction
Predict which incoming payments are likely to require manual intervention based on remittance quality, partial payment patterns and historical exception rates by customer.
Business value: Finance teams prepare for exception volume before the matching run - improving close predictability.
Collections Outreach Recommendations
Prescribe the right collections action per invoice - automated reminder, personal follow-up or escalation to dispute team - based on risk score, account value and relationship history.
Business value: Collections strategy is consistent, evidence-based and applied across every account - not dependent on individual judgement.
Cash Application Agent
Auto-matches incoming payments to open SAP FI invoices using remittance data and pattern matching. Classifies and enriches unmatched exceptions and routes only genuine edge cases to the finance team for manual review.
Business value: Eliminates 60-70% of manual cash matching effort and accelerates the financial close with a full exception audit trail.
Predictive Cash Forecasting
Generate 30, 60 and 90-day cash receipt forecasts by combining AR aging, customer payment patterns, risk scores and open order pipeline - giving the CFO a data-driven view of expected cash position.
Business value: Replaces spreadsheet-based treasury estimates. Finance leadership plans liquidity and working capital deployment on a forecast grounded in live AR signals.
Working Capital Optimisation Recommendations
Identify the highest-impact levers across DSO reduction, collections timing and credit policy adjustment - quantified by expected cash release and ranked by effort - for the finance leadership team to action.
Business value: Working capital decisions move from intuition to evidence. Each recommendation is backed by AR data and shows the expected impact before action is taken.
Cash Position Report Agent
Generates the weekly cash position and O2C health report automatically - pulling actuals from SAP FI, forecast from the predictive model and exceptions from across the chain - and distributes it to the CFO and finance leadership on schedule.
Business value: Eliminates the manual weekly finance reporting cycle. Leadership receives a consistent, data-driven O2C health view without any analyst prep time.
