Agent Mode Deployment

IIA does not require a centralized data platform. Agent Mode applies where clients have both mature SaaS tools AND well-defined, optimized business processes. Decision models run at the edge on the agent runtime, reading process rules, SLAs and governance from the optimized workflow. Faster to deliver, cheaper to run, and unlocks a broader addressable market of SaaS-heavy enterprises.

HR Intelligence Pod

Replaces manual coordination with an automated agent running across Darwinbox, email, SharePoint, ticketing and BGV - from offer acceptance to Day 1.

Employee Onboarding Agent

PREDICTIVE
Offer Acceptance Likelihood
Score each open offer on acceptance probability using role, compensation band, candidate engagement signals and historical patterns.
Business value: Prioritise pre-boarding actions for high-risk offers and avoid last-minute backfill surprises.
BGV Delay Risk Prediction
Predict which background verifications are at risk of delay based on vendor history, candidate profile and geography.
Business value: Flag at-risk cases early and trigger escalation before they threaten the start date.
IT Provisioning Timeline Forecast
Forecast IT ticket completion time based on role, location, current queue depth and hardware availability.
Business value: Identify provisioning bottlenecks before Day 1 and allow proactive reallocation of resources.
PRESCRIPTIVE
Next Best Action for HR Coordinators
Rank actions across all active onboarding cases: which candidate to chase, which ticket to escalate, which step is blocking.
Business value: Replaces manual task tracking with a prioritised action list.
Onboarding Checklist Optimisation
Prescribe the optimal task sequence for each role and location, running steps in parallel where possible to cut total cycle time.
Business value: Shorter onboarding cycles and fewer bottlenecks from sequential handoffs.
Escalation and Exception Guidance
On SLA breach or overdue task, surfaces the escalation path, responsible contact and resolution steps for that exception.
Business value: Consistent, auditable exception handling regardless of coordinator experience.
AGENTIC
Autonomous Onboarding Orchestrator
Monitors offer status, raises IT and admin tickets, initiates BGV and updates Darwinbox at each milestone. No manual coordination.
Business value: Every step fires on time, for every new hire, without a coordinator chasing it.
Intelligent SLA Watchdog
Tracks all open tasks against SLA thresholds, sends escalation alerts to responsible parties and logs breach events.
Business value: No manual chasing. No new hire falls through the cracks.
Day 1 Readiness Confirmation Agent
Validates completion of all prerequisites across systems, generates a readiness report and notifies the hiring manager and HR coordinator for sign-off.
Business value: Every new hire arrives prepared. Full audit trail included.
